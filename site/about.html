About Us

Welcome to the Polio Awareness Web Application. Our mission is to raise awareness about polio and provide valuable resources to help combat this debilitating disease.

We are a team of dedicated individuals who are passionate about public health and the eradication of polio. Our goal is to educate, inform, and empower users to take action against polio by providing comprehensive information and interactive tools.

Our application serves as an educational platform where users can learn about polio prevention, symptoms, and vaccination efforts. We believe that through knowledge and awareness, we can make a significant impact in the fight against polio.

We invite you to explore our application, utilize the resources provided, and join us in our mission to eradicate polio worldwide.
