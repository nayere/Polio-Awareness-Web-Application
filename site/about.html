About Us

Welcome to the Polio Awareness Web Application. Our mission is to raise awareness about polio and provide valuable resources to help combat this debilitating disease.

We are a team of dedicated individuals who are passionate about public health and the eradication of polio. Our goal is to educate, inform, and empower users to take action against polio by providing comprehensive information and interactive tools.

Our application serves as an educational platform where users can learn about polio prevention, symptoms, and vaccination efforts. We believe that through knowledge and awareness, we can make a significant impact in the fight against polio.

We invite you to explore our application, utilize the resources provided, and join us in our mission to eradicate polio worldwide.

Project Inspiration

The inspiration behind this project stems from a personal journey and the challenges faced by those around us. Growing up in Africa, the stigma around mental health has always been profound. Many individuals suffer in silence, believing that discussing their mental health issues is not culturally acceptable. This project was born out of a desire to change that narrative and provide a platform for people to take control of their mental well-being.

The journey began when I, Job nayere, witnessed a close friend's struggle with mental health issues. The lack of accessible mental health resources and the prevailing stigma made it difficult for them to seek help. This experience ignited a passion within me to create a tool that could bridge the gap and offer support to those in need.

This project is also a Portfolio Project for Holberton School. As part of our curriculum, we are encouraged to develop practical solutions to real-world problems, and this web application is a testament to that mission.
